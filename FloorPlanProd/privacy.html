Privacy Policy for FormaPlan
Effective Date: 05/08/2025

FormaPlan (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application, FormaPlan, developed with Unity.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you register or use the app:
- Your name
- Your email address

This information is used for authentication, user account management, and communication.

b. Usage Data
We may automatically collect data such as:
- Device type and operating system
- IP address (may be anonymized)
- App interaction and crash data
- Session duration and analytics data

c. Location Data
We may collect approximate location (e.g., via IP address) for analytics purposes. We do not collect precise location data without explicit permission.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate users and manage accounts (via Firebase)
- Provide and maintain app functionality
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze anonymized usage patterns
- Communicate support or service updates when needed

3. Third-Party Services
FormaPlan integrates the following third-party services:
- Firebase Authentication – for secure login and account management
- Firebase Analytics – for anonymized usage tracking and diagnostics

These services may collect data according to their own policies. For more information, please refer to the Firebase Privacy Policy:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Data Retention
We retain your name and email address as long as your account is active or as needed to operate the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. All communication with Firebase is encrypted in transit and at rest.

6. Children’s Privacy
FormaPlan is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us for removal.

7. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access, update, or delete your personal data
- Deactivate or delete your account
- Withdraw consent to data processing

Contact us below to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective Date” above. Continued use of the app signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
